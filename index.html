About Us

Our Mission,Vision,History and team.

Our Mision

Jesus said, " I am come that they might have life, and that they might have it more abundantly. "(John 10:10).  Our mission is, therefore, to lead others to Jesus Christ through our digital platforms, and to help them find:  firstly, peace and happiness in this life and secondly,assurance of their salvation and life eternal through Jesus Christ, our Lord and Saviour.

Our Vision

Uzima Milele Ministry, through its strong team of evangelists, pastors, medicalmissionaries, ICT consultants and partners, brings life-changing knowledge to the palm of your hands.Anyone, Anywhere, Anytime will be able to access bible studies, general health and family life education ONLINE, through our digital platforms.We pray, and believe, that through Uzima Milele, millions may find peace and joy in this life, and the knowledge they need to gain life eternal.

Our History

Uzima Milele is a not-for-profit Christian Ministry that provides Biblical, Health &Social Education through Digital Platforms in the Swahili language, targeting over 100 million people residing in the Swahili-speaking Sub-Saharan countries.

Our Amazing Team

Our amazing team of volunteers, who are mainly evangelists, ICT, healthcare, and project professionals, are committed to helping others. We take our convictions and turn them into action. Think you would be a good fit? Get in touch for more information!
